Inferential Statistics

Inferential Statistics is a branch of statistics which uses the results obtained from the descriptive statistics to make inferences and draw conclusions from them. This Inferential Statistics is mainly used for Testing of Hypothesis, Analysing the data, Data Analysis, Business Analysis, Scientific Research etc.,


Various measures in the Inferential Statistics are as follows:


  1. Sampling
  2. Estimation
  3. Hypothesis Testing
  4. Confidence Intervels
  5. Analysis of Varience
  6. Regression Analysis
  7. Chi-square test

The aim of Inferential Statistics is to draw the conclusions from the given data to improve the performence of the business fo Industries and to take precautions regarding the diseases for Hospitals, Time to Time monitoring of Public Health by the Government and many more.
