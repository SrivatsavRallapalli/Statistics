Descriptive Statistics


Descriptive Statistics is a branch of statistics which gives the overview and summarize the whole dataset to a single value. It deals with the collection, analysis and presentation of the data using visualization. Most of the fields uses this Descriptive statistics for the future growth. This Descriptive Statistics again branched into many types.


Branches of Descriptive Statistics are as follows:


  1. Measures of Central Tendency
  2. Measures of Dispersion
  3. Measures of Shape
  4. Frequency Distribution
  5. Percentile and Quartile
  6. Graphical Representation

But with the help of the Descriptivce statistics we cannot draw any conclusions for the given data. It is useful only to summarize the data.
